Drills

Master the basics, dominate the field. Here's how.

🔄 Ball Mastery

Drill: 10 Touch Ball Control
✅ Inside touches, toe taps, sole rolls
💡 Why? Improves rhythm, balance, and control.

🌀 Dribbling

Drill: Cone Maze
✅ Quick feet between cones, tight turns
💡 Helps you beat defenders.

Repetition creates instinct.

🧱 Passing

Drill: Wall Pass Reps
✅ Pass & receive against a wall, both feet
💡 Builds technique and accuracy.

🎯 Shooting

Drill: 1-Touch Finishes
✅ Pass from friend → 1-touch shot on goal
💡 Focuses on quick finishing under pressure.

💡 Coach’s Tip

Start slow. Speed comes after control.
